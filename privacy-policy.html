Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20/11/2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

This Privacy Policy explains how Loanlav (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when users visit our website. Loanlav is an informational and comparison platform designed to help users explore personal loan options and related financial services offered by third-party providers.

We do not collect, request, or store personal information directly from visitors. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Financial or banking details
  • Account or login information

Our website does not include contact forms, registration forms, or lead-capture systems that require users to submit personal data directly to Loanlav.

2. Third-Party Websites & External Links

Loanlav may include links to third-party websites such as loan discovery platforms, financial service providers, or other external resources.

Please note:

  • When you click a third-party link, you leave the Loanlav website.
  • We do not control the privacy practices, cookies, or data collection methods of third-party websites.
  • Any information you provide on third-party websites is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before sharing personal information.

3. Cookies & Anonymous Analytics

Loanlav may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or server-level logging) to understand how visitors interact with our website.

These tools may collect non-personal, anonymous information such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Approximate geographic location (city or region level only)
  • Time spent on pages

This information is used solely to improve website performance, content quality, and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored by Loanlav.

4. No Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

Because Loanlav does not collect personal data, we do not:

  • Sell personal information
  • Share personal information
  • Rent or trade user data
  • Track users across websites for behavioral profiling

5. Affiliate & Advertising Disclosure

Loanlav may promote, compare, or review third-party financial services and loan discovery platforms. We may earn a commission if users click outbound links and engage with third-party providers.

This does not affect loan terms, pricing, approval decisions, or user experience. Loanlav does not act as a lender, broker, or financial institution.

6. Children’s Privacy

Loanlav does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Our website and content are intended for a general audience and are not directed toward minors.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect website improvements, legal requirements, or regulatory updates. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how Loanlav operates, you may contact us using any publicly listed contact method available on our website.

What Drives Us

At Loanlav, we believe access to clear information is the foundation of smart financial decision-making. Our platform brings together research-driven content, comparisons, and educational resources designed to help users navigate personal loan options with confidence.

We aim to simplify the discovery process by highlighting meaningful differences between platforms, explaining how credit profiles may affect eligibility, and outlining what users can expect at each step of the journey. Loanlav does not act as a lender or broker; our role is to inform, educate, and support users as they explore potential financial solutions offered by third parties.By staying focused on accuracy and relevance, Loanlav helps users stay informed without pressure—so every decision is made with clarity and confidence.
